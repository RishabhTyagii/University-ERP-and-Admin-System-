Step-by-Step Admission Guide
1
Eligibility Check & Course Selection

SVSU University portal par visit karein aur apni qualification ke anusar course eligibility check karein. Available programmes aur unki eligibility criteria carefully review karein.

2
Application Form Fill Karein

Online admission inquiry/application form bharen. Saare personal, academic aur contact details accurately fill karein.

3
Required Documents Submit Karein

Saare required documents ki scanned copies upload karein ya university office mein jama karein (dekhein document list neeche).

4
Document Verification

University/Admin team dwara documents ki verification ki jaayegi. Agar koi additional document required hoga toh aapko contact kiya jaayega.

5
Fee Submission

Approval milne ke baad prescribed fee online ya demand draft ke through submit karein. Fee structure dekhein →

6
Admission Confirmed – Enrollment Generation

Fee submission ke baad aapka enrollment number generate hoga aur student ERP portal access milega. Welcome to SVSU University!
